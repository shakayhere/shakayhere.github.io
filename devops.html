Building Infrastructure as Code

With cloud computing, the number of infrastructure components has grown, more applications are being released to production on a daily basis, and infrastructure needs to be able to be spun up, scaled, and taken down frequently. Infrastructure as Code (IaC) can help an organization manage IT infrastructure needs while also improving consistency and reducing errors and manual configuration.

Maintaining CI/CD Pipelines

CI/CD is the backbone of a DevOps methodology, bringing developers and IT operations teams together to deploy software. Maintaining CI/CD according to development needs brings value to the organization which includes removal of manual errors, providing standardized feedback loops to developers, and enabling fast product iterations.

Auto-Scaling to new Horizons

Autoscaling enables organizations to scale their cloud services up or down automatically based on defined situations such as traffic utilization levels. This makes your application robust, fault tolerant and highly available with better cost management.

Developing Strategies for Devs and Ops

In order for an organization to work with blazing speed, communication between its engineers should be efficient. I make strategies that allign development and operations teams through a DevOps approach that leads to fewer errors, automated deployments, and less inconsistencies.

Monitoring Performance & Setting Alerts

The ultimate benefit of monitoring performance of your application is to better predict, resolve, and prevent issues. It allows engineers to optimize application for better performance. Adding alerts to specific metrics can be used to identify security threats or application crashes. This makes possible for businesses to grow exponentially.

Reducing & Optimising Cloud Costs

Cloud cost optimization is not just about cutting costs — it's about knowing where to spend your money to maximize the business value. Measuring performance and allocating cloud resources on-demand is an iterative and continuous process that should be handled with care to increase productivity and business agility.
